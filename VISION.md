# Vision — Orqestly

## Core Idea

Software should be validated against **intent**, not just implementation.

---

## Problem

Testing today is:
- manual
- incomplete
- disconnected from requirements

---

## Future

Testing becomes:

- autonomous
- continuous
- requirement-driven

---

## Orqestly Mission

To eliminate the gap between:

> What was intended vs what actually works

---

## End State

A system where:

- requirements are input
- validation is automatic
- defects are generated without human effort

---

## Long-Term Impact

- Reduced QA cost
- Faster releases
- Higher software reliability
